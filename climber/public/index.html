CLIMBER

Two-handed climber. Use your ears.

Language

    How to Play

    Controls

    • A — left hand reaches up.
    • Z — left hand drops down (also dodges left-side pots).
    • K — right hand reaches up.
    • M — right hand drops down (also dodges right-side pots).
    • Esc — pause / quit.
    • F1 floor, F2 score, F3 lives, F4 hand status.

    Climbing

    • Both hands grip windows. Alternate hands to climb: A, then K, then A again, and so on.
    • Hands cannot be more than one floor apart. The lower hand must catch up before the higher one can move further.
    • Each ear plays the corresponding hand's grip tone. Pitch rises with altitude.

    Hazards

    • Falling pots whistle from above on the left or the right. Press the matching DOWN key (Z or M) to dodge.
    • Windows can close on you. A creak warns you a second before. Move that hand quickly or you fall.
    • High floors hold a gorilla. Its roar throbs in the centre. When it swipes (whoosh), drop both hands at once.

    Learn the Sounds

    Audition each cue. Use arrow keys to focus, Enter to play.
